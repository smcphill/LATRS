sweep(time_ago = nil)

invoke with: +./script/runner -e [development|test|production] "Session.sweep();"+ time_ago can be [0-9][mhd], where m = minutes, h = hours and d = days. this will determine how old a session has to be for it to be destroyed. the default is 1 day. however, all sessions created more than a week ago will always be destroyed
